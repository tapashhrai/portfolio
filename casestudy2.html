PROBLEM

Buying ticket at movie theater counter takes lot of time especially when movie is doing good. It is very tedious for user to stay in line for hours to get ticke and this make people angry and sometime they have to return. Inorder to reduce time, this webiste "Movie Seat Reservation" allow quicker way to purchase tickets online and reserve seats by staying at home.

THE GOAL

Design a website for seat reservation for movie theater that allow users to book ticket and reserve seat online.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer. [UI (user interface), UX (user experience)]

Responsibilities

User research, User persona, Problem statement, User journey map, Competitive audit, Ideation activitie, User flow, Storyboard, Low fidelity wireframe, Low fidelity prototypes, High fidelity mockups, High fidelity prototypes, Usability studies

UNDERSTANDING THE USER

User research: Summary

Conducted user interviews to better understand the target and user needs. After conducting the user interviews, the primary users group was working adults who wants to take a break from busy life and spend quality time with family and friend. They do not want to do step by step process where they have to stay on line to book the ticket.

User Research: Pain Point

01

Solve easy online booking

Waiting in-line at movie theater counter ruins the moment of quality time spending with family or friend.

02

N clear info

Users were confuse how they will know if they have booked the seat or not.


03

No Accessbility

No assistive technology to support all kinds of people

DESIGN

Paper wireframe

I sketched paper wireframe of homepage, upload, login and signup page to explore various options for how to organize the information. I made notes on each screen of what I thought worked best, and incorporated those ideas into the digital low fidelity wireframes.



Digital wireframe

Next, I moved to Figma to create digital wireframes for all of the screens. Using a basic design system, I was able to create low fidelity wireframes that were still clean and organized. The homepage serves as a central hub where users can access a quick dashboard. The navigation gives users go thou different movies, search for movies and book ticket and res seat online.
